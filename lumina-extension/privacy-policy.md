Privacy Policy for Lumina Collector

Last Updated: [10/12/2025]

1. Data Collection Lumina Collector does not collect personally identifiable information (such as names, emails, or addresses). The extension handles the following data to perform its core function:
  - Pantry ID: A unique identifier provided by the user to sync flashcards with their specific Lumina deck.
  - User Selections: The specific text highlighted by the user on a webpage when they choose "Add to Lumina Deck."
2. Data Usage We use the data described above solely to provide the extension's functionality:
  - Pantry ID: Saved locally in your browser's Chrome Sync storage (chrome.storage.sync) to allow the extension to identify where to save your flashcards.
  - User Selections: Sent momentarily to the Free Dictionary API to fetch definitions and then sent to Pantry Cloud to update your flashcard deck.
3. Third-Party Services Lumina Collector interacts with the following third-party services. We do not control these services, and data sent to them is subject to their respective policies:
  - Free Dictionary API (dictionaryapi.dev): We send the word you highlight to this service to retrieve its definition.
  - Pantry Cloud (getpantry.cloud): We send the word, definition, and your Pantry ID to this service to store and sync your flashcards.
4. Data Storage Your Pantry ID is stored securely within your browser using Chrome's native storage API. We do not maintain our own servers or databases to store your information.
5. Contact If you have questions about this policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
